Halibut is a delicious and versatile fish that can be prepared in a variety of ways. Here are some delicious halibut dishes you might enjoy:
Grilled Halibut with Mango Salsa: Season halibut fillets with salt and pepper, and grill them until cooked through. Top with a fresh salsa made with diced mango, 
red onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and salt.

Baked Halibut with Lemon and Herbs: Preheat oven to 375°F. Place halibut fillets in a baking dish, and season with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs (such 
as dill or parsley). Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil, and bake for 15-20 minutes.

Halibut Chowder: Sauté chopped onions, celery, and garlic in butter until soft. Add diced potatoes and chicken broth, and simmer until potatoes are tender. Add 
halibut chunks and corn, and cook until fish is opaque. Stir in heavy cream and chopped parsley before serving.

Pan-Seared Halibut with Caper Sauce: Season halibut fillets with salt and pepper, and sear them in a hot pan until browned and cooked through. Remove from pan and 
keep warm. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Add capers, lemon juice, and white wine, and simmer until sauce thickens slightly. 
Serve sauce over halibut.

Halibut Tacos: Season halibut fillets with cumin, chili powder, and salt, and grill or sauté until cooked through. Serve in warm tortillas with shredded cabbage, 
avocado, salsa, and lime wedges.

Enjoy your halibut dishes!
