There's no one secret to happiness, as what brings happiness can vary greatly from person to person. However, some common things that can contribute to happiness include:

Strong relationships with friends and family

A sense of purpose or meaning in life

Engaging in activities that you enjoy and that help you relax

Setting and working towards personal goals

Taking care of your physical and mental health

Expressing gratitude and being kind to others

It's important to find what works for you and to make an effort to incorporate those things into your life.
