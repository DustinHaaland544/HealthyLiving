Here are some ways you can try to manifest positive energy:

- Practice gratitude: Make an effort to focus on the things you are grateful for in your life. This can help shift your perspective and focus your attention on the 
  positive.

- Set positive intentions: Choose to focus on positive outcomes and intentions in your daily life. This can help you attract more positive experiences and opportunities.

- Surround yourself with positive people: The people you spend time with can have a big impact on your energy and mood. Surround yourself with people who are positive and 
  supportive.

- Take care of yourself: Make sure to prioritize your physical and mental well-being. Eat well, exercise, and make time for activities that help you relax and recharge.

- Practice acts of kindness: Helping others and being kind can boost your own sense of well-being and positivity.

- Find activities that bring you joy: Make time for hobbies, activities, and experiences that bring you happiness and a sense of fulfillment.

- Remember, it's important to be patient and persistent in your efforts to manifest positive energy. It takes time and effort to cultivate positivity, but it can be a 
  rewarding and enriching experience.
